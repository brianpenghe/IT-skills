# Skill: Update Google Maps Work Address on iPhone

## Purpose

Update the saved **Work** address in the current Google Maps iOS app.

## Verified Workflow

1.  Open **Google Maps**.
2.  Tap your **profile picture**.
3.  Tap **Your data in Maps**.
4.  Under **Related info and controls**, tap **Home and work
    addresses**.
5.  Tap **Work**.
6.  Edit the address.
7.  Tap **Save**.
8.  **Force-close Google Maps and reopen it.**

## Notes

-   The current iOS interface places **Home and work addresses** under
    **Related info and controls**.
-   Google documents editing the address, but does **not** explicitly
    require restarting the app.
-   In practice, restarting Google Maps helps refresh cached Home/Work
    labels and ensures the updated address is reflected throughout the
    app.
