# Skill: Switching App Store / Media & Purchases Accounts (iOS)

## Objective
How to sign out of one regional Apple ID (e.g., American/US) and sign into another regional Apple ID (e.g., English/UK) on the App Store without changing the region of a single account.

## Steps to Switch Accounts

1. **Open Settings:** Open the main **Settings** app on your iPhone or iPad.
2. **Access Apple Account:** Tap your **Name / Apple Account** banner at the very top of the screen.
3. **Select Media & Purchases:** Tap **Media & Purchases**.
4. **Sign Out:** Tap **Sign Out** from the pop-up menu that appears. (This only affects App Store/iTunes media and leaves your main iCloud/phone settings intact).
5. **Sign In:** Once it shows as signed out, tap **Media & Purchases** again (it may say "Turn On" or "Continue").
6. **Use Different Credentials:** Tap **"Not [Your Name]?"** (or choose to use a different account) and enter the Apple ID username and password for your other regional account (the English/UK account).

Once you sign in with the new credentials, the App Store will automatically refresh its region, purchases, and app availability to match the newly active account.
